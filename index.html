INPUT

Ingest and normalize approved legal sources from the public ecosystem.

RETRIEVAL

02. Index

Retrieve candidate evidence with the lowest possible ambiguity.

VERIFY

03. Review

Keep ambiguous relations in human review until they can be defended with evidence.

OUTPUT

Lex

Grounded legal reasoning that abstains when the record is weak.

Fail-Closed Logic

If there is no strong evidence in the canonical sources, the system abstains. That is a feature, not a failure.

Bounded Scope

The public story is anchored in a few clear domains, not in inflated claims of universal legal coverage.

Graph of Relations

The goal is not generic text search. The goal is a verifiable network of sources, relations, and review states.
